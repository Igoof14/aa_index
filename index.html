Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our furniture cleaning services.

We recommend having your upholstery professionally cleaned every 12-18 months for optimal maintenance. High-traffic furniture or homes with pets/children may benefit from more frequent cleaning every 6-12 months.

While we can remove most common stains, some may be permanent depending on the type of stain, fabric, and how long it's been set. Our technicians will evaluate each stain and use specialized treatments to achieve the best possible results.

Drying times vary based on fabric type, humidity, and airflow. Typically, upholstery dries in 4-6 hours, while deeper cleaning may take 6-12 hours. We use powerful extraction equipment to minimize moisture and speed drying time.
